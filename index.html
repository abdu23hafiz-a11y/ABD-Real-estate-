A-102What's included

Everything a landlord actually touches.

No modules to bolt on later. Every plan includes the four things that take up your week.

Rent collection

Tenants pay online from their portal. You see who's paid, who's late, and what's owed across every unit — automatically.

Maintenance requests

Tenants submit a request with photos in seconds. You assign it to a vendor and track it through to done, in one place.

Tenant communication

One message thread per unit for announcements, lease renewals, and questions — no more digging through old emails.

Financial reporting

Income, expenses, and occupancy by property or across your whole portfolio. Export it whenever your accountant asks.

A-103How it works

Up and running in an afternoon.

No data migration team required.

STEP 1

Add your properties

Import your units, leases, and tenant contacts from a spreadsheet — or start fresh in a few minutes per unit.

STEP 2

Invite your tenants

Each tenant gets a portal for rent, requests, and documents. They set it up themselves; you don't.

STEP 3

Let it run

Reminders, late fees, and monthly reports happen on their own. You step in only when something needs you.
